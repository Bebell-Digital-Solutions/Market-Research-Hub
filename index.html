⏳ Market Research Timeline

Phase 1
Week 1-2
Market Investigation
  • Define your target market and customer personas
  • Analyze market size and growth potential
  • Identify key market trends and dynamics
  • Conduct SWOT analysis of the market
Phase 2
Week 3-4
Trend & Niche Identification
  • Identify emerging trends in your industry
  • Discover profitable niche opportunities
  • Analyze search trends and social media buzz
  • Validate demand through keyword research
Phase 3
Week 5-6
Competitive Analysis
  • Identify direct and indirect competitors
  • Analyze competitor strengths and weaknesses
  • Map competitor pricing and positioning
  • Identify gaps in the competitive landscape
Phase 4
Week 7
Product Validation
  • Validate product-market fit
  • Test pricing strategies
  • Gather early customer feedback
  • Conduct pre-launch surveys
Phase 5
Week 8
Advanced Analysis & Strategy
  • Compile research findings into actionable insights
  • Develop go-to-market strategy
  • Create marketing and positioning recommendations
  • Finalize research documentation
